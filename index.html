Our Services

Custom Furniture Design

We offer bespoke furniture design services to meet your specific needs and preferences. Our skilled designers work closely with you to create pieces that perfectly fit your space and style. Whether you need a custom dining table, a unique sofa, or a tailored wardrobe, we bring your vision to life with meticulous craftsmanship.

Furniture Restoration

Our expert craftsmen can restore your old furniture to its former glory. We handle everything from minor repairs to complete restorations, preserving the original beauty and functionality of your cherished pieces. Our restoration process includes detailed assessments, material sourcing, and skillful workmanship to ensure the best results.

Consultation Services

We provide professional consultation to help you choose the right furniture for your space. Our experts offer personalized advice on design, layout, and materials to ensure that your furniture enhances both the functionality and aesthetic appeal of your home or office.

Interior Design Assistance

Our interior design team can assist you in creating a cohesive and stylish look for your entire home. From selecting the right furniture pieces to choosing color schemes and accessories, we help you transform your space into a harmonious and inviting environment.

Space Planning

Maximize the potential of your space with our expert space planning services. We analyze your room layout and suggest furniture arrangements that optimize flow and functionality. Whether you have a small apartment or a large office, we help you make the most of your available space.

About Us

At Aman Furniture, we believe that your home is your sanctuary. Established in 2000, we have spent over two decades crafting furniture that not only meets the highest standards of quality and durability but also adds a touch of elegance and comfort to your living spaces. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create furniture that combines functionality with beauty, ensuring that every piece we produce becomes a beloved part of your home.

Our skilled craftsmen use only the finest materials, from sustainably sourced wood to premium fabrics, ensuring that every item is built to last. We take pride in our meticulous attention to detail and our commitment to excellence, which is evident in every stitch, joint, and finish. Our product range includes a diverse selection of furniture for every room in your home, from luxurious living room sets and elegant bedroom furniture to practical office solutions and stylish outdoor pieces.
